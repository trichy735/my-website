Biography

Dietrich Stogner is the author of the fantasy horror novel "Frostbitten." With a passion for writing and indie publishing, Dietrich shares insights and tips on his TikTok channel, where he helps aspiring writers navigate the world of self-publishing. His engaging storytelling and dedication to his craft have earned him a dedicated following.
